Automatically enable and diable touchpad when (dis-)connecting USB mouse via udev

Fri 21 October 2016

Motivation

I own a laptop with a big touchpad. When an external mouse is plugged in, I disable the touchpad so that I don't use it accidentally (e.g. while typing or gaming). When taking the laptop with me, I don't also carry my mouse but use the touchpad. Which means I have to enable it again. These cumbersome steps take some time I would like to automate.

alias disable_touchpad='xinput set-prop 14 "Synaptics Off" 1'
